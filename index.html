the web aint hard The Web Aint Hard At Patreon we're getting an opportunity to refactor an web application that serves at lot of people! So in that process we've hired people who've worked on all sorts of web applications: Angular, React, and Ember being the most popular. At the end of the day these are all just tools. We'll explore how to simplify your toolbelt and deploy a realtime static html app in minutes. - Utilizing services for infrastructure - Sane Frontend/JavaScript development w/ NPM and CommonJS modules - Focus on solving your domain problem, not maintaining it - Hosting and realtime data storage for cheap By the end you'll be saying "The Web Aint Hard".
